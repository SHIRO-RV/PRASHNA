About Prana

Prana is the vital life force in Vedic philosophy that sustains all living beings. It flows through every part of the body and mind, and its balance is essential for well-being.

The 9 Planets (Navagrahas) & Dasha Periods

The Vimshottari Dasha system is based on the influence of 9 celestial bodies, with their respective Dasha periods:

  • Sun: 6 years
  • Moon: 10 years
  • Mars: 7 years
  • Rahu: 18 years
  • Jupiter: 16 years
  • Saturn: 19 years
  • Mercury: 17 years
  • Ketu: 7 years
  • Venus: 20 years

The 27 Nakshatras

The zodiac in Vedic astrology is divided into 27 Nakshatras (lunar mansions), each with unique characteristics. They are: Aswini, Bharani, Krittika, Rohini, Mrigashira, Ardra, Punarvasu, Pushya, Ashlesha, Magha, Purva Phalguni, Uttara Phalguni, Hasta, Chitra, Swati, Vishakha, Anuradha, Jyeshtha, Mula, Purva Ashadha, Uttara Ashadha, Shravana, Dhanishta, Shatabhisha, Purva Bhadrapada, Uttara Bhadrapada, and Revati.
